OpenAI Organizational Chart

A comprehensive overview of OpenAI's current organizational structure, leadership team, and recent departures.

Based on publicly available information as of March 2025

Corporate Structure

OpenAI operates under a unique dual structure:

  • OpenAI, Inc. - The non-profit entity that governs the organization
  • OpenAI LP - The capped-profit arm that handles commercial operations

Note: OpenAI is planning a transition to a public benefit corporation (PBC) structure in 2025.

Organizational Model

OpenAI employs a unique two-layer organizational structure:

  • Leadership team and board - Executive leadership providing strategic direction
  • Flat employee structure - No middle management, with employees organized into autonomous "pods"

Pod Structure

OpenAI uses approximately 80 pods with 475 engineers total. A typical pod includes:

  • 1 Pod Owner (Technical Lead)
  • 2-3 Senior Engineers
  • 1-2 ML Specialists
  • 1 Quality Assurance Expert

Major Departments & Teams

Applied Engineering

Led by Srinivas Narayanan (VP of Applied), this team is responsible for developing and maintaining OpenAI's products, including ChatGPT and APIs.

Safety Systems

Responsible for ensuring AI models can be safely deployed, this team conducts safety assessments and develops methods such as safety reward models and multimodal classifiers.

Research

OpenAI's research teams focus on various aspects of AI development, including safety reasoning, robustness & safety training, multimodal safety, and trustworthy AI.

Security

Handles data center security, network security, identity management, and access control to protect OpenAI's infrastructure and intellectual property.
